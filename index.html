About Us

Treat yourself to an amazing poolside view with babbling fountains by the side.

As you step into the majestic and serene surroundings, a splendid entrance lined with timeless lanterns, finely crafted stone sculptures, and elegant date palms extends a warm invitation.

The resort occupies seven acres of land, and natural stones carved and polished by skilled hands serve as one of the primary building elements. This symbolizes the intense connection between Mother Nature & Humankind. Experience a close look at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium from our Terrace and be enchanted by the magnificent Gazebos within the pool.
